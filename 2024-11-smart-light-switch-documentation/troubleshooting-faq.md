# Smart Light Switch - Troubleshooting & FAQ

## Common Issues
- **Device offline**: Check Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz only). Restart router or re-pair.
- **No response to touch**: Check power and wiring. Reset device.
- **Dimming not working**: Bulb must be dimmable LED.
- **Flickering lights**: Incompatible bulb or loose wiring.
