# Physical Installation

This section describes how to physically install the device prior to electrical connection and configuration.

Installation must be performed by qualified personnel familiar with industrial equipment and local safety regulations.

---

## Installation Requirements

Before installing the device, ensure that:
- the installation surface is stable and level
- sufficient space is available for ventilation and cable access
- environmental conditions meet the specified operating limits

Refer to the Technical Specifications appendix for detailed requirements.

---

## Mounting the Device

The device is designed for fixed installation.

### Mounting Steps

1. Identify the mounting location according to system design requirements.
2. Secure the mounting bracket or enclosure using appropriate fasteners.
3. Verify that the device is firmly attached and does not move under light force.
4. Ensure that all ports and indicators remain accessible after installation.

Improper mounting may result in mechanical stress or unreliable operation.

---

## Orientation and Clearance

Install the device in the recommended orientation to ensure proper cooling.

Maintain adequate clearance around the device:
- to allow airflow
- to prevent heat buildup
- to enable maintenance access

Do not obstruct ventilation openings.

---

## Pre-Power Inspection

Before applying power:
- confirm that the device is securely mounted
- inspect the enclosure for visible damage
- verify that no foreign objects are present inside the enclosure

Do not proceed with electrical connection if any issues are observed.
