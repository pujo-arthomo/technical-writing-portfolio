# Glossary

This glossary defines technical terms and abbreviations used throughout this manual.

Terms are listed alphabetically for ease of reference.

---

**Calibration**  
The process of adjusting a measurement system to ensure accuracy.

---

**Configuration**  
The act of setting software or hardware parameters to define system behavior.

---

**Diagnostic Message**  
A system-generated message indicating status, warnings, or faults.

---

**Firmware**  
Embedded software that controls device operation.

---

**Grounding**  
The connection of equipment to earth to ensure electrical safety and stability.

---

**Operational State**  
A defined condition representing the current mode of device operation.

---

**Sensor Module**  
A hardware component used to interface with external measurement sources.

---

**System Initialization**  
The startup process during which the system verifies hardware and loads configuration settings.

---

**Warning**  
An indication of a condition that may affect operation or safety if not addressed.

---

## Glossary Usage Notes

Definitions in this glossary are specific to this manual and device context.

If a term is not listed, refer to standard industry definitions or related documentation.
