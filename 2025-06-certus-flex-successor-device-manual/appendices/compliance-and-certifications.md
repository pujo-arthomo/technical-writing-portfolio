# Compliance and Certifications

This appendix lists compliance information and certifications applicable to the device.

Compliance ensures that the device meets applicable safety, electromagnetic, and environmental requirements.

---

## Regulatory Compliance

The device is designed to comply with relevant regional and international regulations.

Compliance may include:
- electrical safety standards
- electromagnetic compatibility requirements
- environmental directives

Applicable regulations depend on the region of installation.

---

## Safety Standards

The device conforms to recognized safety standards for industrial equipment.

These standards address:
- electrical safety
- mechanical safety
- safe operation under specified conditions

Installation and operation must follow documented guidelines to maintain compliance.

---

## Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC)

The device is designed to operate within specified electromagnetic limits.

To maintain EMC compliance:
- use approved cables and connectors
- follow recommended grounding practices
- avoid unauthorized modifications

Improper installation may reduce EMC performance.

---

## Environmental Compliance

The device may comply with environmental directives related to:
- material restrictions
- recycling requirements
- disposal procedures

Dispose of the device according to local regulations.

---

## Certification Identification

Certification markings are located on the device label.

Do not remove or obscure certification labels.

For detailed certification documentation, contact the manufacturer or authorized representative.
