# Maintenance Procedures

This section describes routine maintenance activities required to ensure reliable device operation.

Maintenance must be performed by qualified personnel following site safety procedures.

---

## Maintenance Overview

Regular maintenance helps:
- prevent unexpected failures
- maintain measurement accuracy
- extend device service life

Maintenance intervals may vary depending on operating conditions.

---

## Routine Inspection

Perform routine inspections to:
- check physical condition
- verify secure mounting
- inspect wiring and connectors
- ensure ventilation paths are clear

Address any issues immediately.

---

## Cleaning Procedures

When cleaning the device:
- power off the system
- use dry or slightly damp cloths
- avoid solvents or abrasive materials

Ensure the device is completely dry before restoring power.

---

## Sensor Module Maintenance

Sensor modules may require periodic inspection or replacement.

When servicing modules:
- power off the device
- follow proper handling procedures
- verify correct seating after reinstallation

Reconfigure sensors if required after replacement.

---

## Firmware and Configuration Maintenance

Periodically:
- verify firmware version
- review configuration settings
- back up configuration data

Perform updates only when the system is not in critical operation.

---

## Maintenance Records

Document all maintenance activities, including:
- date and time
- performed actions
- observed issues
- replaced components

Accurate records support long-term system reliability and compliance.
