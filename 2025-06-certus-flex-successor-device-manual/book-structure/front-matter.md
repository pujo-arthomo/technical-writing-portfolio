# Front Matter

## Purpose of This Manual

This manual provides comprehensive operating instructions for the CERTUS Flex successor device.

It is intended for:
- system integrators
- field technicians
- operators
- maintenance personnel

The manual covers device installation, configuration, operation, diagnostics, and maintenance.

---

## Intended Use

The device is designed for industrial monitoring and automation applications.

Use of the device outside the conditions specified in this manual may result in malfunction or unsafe operation.

---

## Document Scope

This manual describes standard device behavior and supported configurations.

Custom implementations or third-party integrations are outside the scope of this document.
