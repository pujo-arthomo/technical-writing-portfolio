# Conventions and Safety Information

This section explains the conventions used throughout this manual and outlines essential safety information.

All users must read and understand this section before installing or operating the device.

---

## Document Conventions

The following conventions are used consistently throughout this manual:

- **Bold text** indicates user interface elements, buttons, or labels.
- *Italic text* highlights emphasis or introduces new terms.
- Monospaced text is used for identifiers, values, or configuration names.
- Numbered lists indicate sequential procedures.
- Bulleted lists indicate non-sequential information.

Cross-references are provided to guide readers to related sections.

---

## Symbols and Indicators

The manual uses standardized symbols to highlight important information:

- **Note**  
  Provides additional information or clarification.

- **Caution**  
  Indicates a situation that could result in equipment damage or data loss.

- **Warning**  
  Indicates a situation that could result in personal injury or unsafe operation.

These indicators must be observed at all times.

---

## Safety Responsibilities

The device must be installed, configured, and operated only by qualified personnel.

Users are responsible for:
- following all safety instructions
- complying with local regulations
- ensuring proper grounding and power connections

Failure to follow safety guidelines may result in malfunction or hazardous conditions.

---

## Electrical Safety

Before connecting power:
- verify voltage and current ratings
- ensure proper grounding
- inspect cables and connectors for damage

Do not connect or disconnect wiring while the device is powered.

---

## Mechanical Safety

When installing or servicing the device:
- secure the device to prevent movement
- avoid contact with sharp edges
- use appropriate tools and protective equipment

Ensure sufficient clearance for ventilation and access.

---

## Operational Safety

During operation:
- monitor status indicators regularly
- do not modify device settings outside documented procedures
- stop operation immediately if abnormal behavior is observed

Unauthorized modifications may compromise safety and device reliability.

---

## Intended Use and Misuse

The device is designed for use only as described in this manual.

Using the device for purposes outside its intended scope may result in:
- unpredictable behavior
- reduced accuracy
- safety risks

The manufacturer is not responsible for damage resulting from improper use.
