# Creating Your First Journal Entry

This article walks through logging your first trade in the journal.

## Step 1: Open the Trade Entry Form
From the dashboard, select the option to add a new trade.

## Step 2: Enter Trade Details
Provide the instrument, entry price, exit price, and trade direction.

Only required fields must be completed to save the entry.

## Step 3: Save the Entry
Once saved, the trade will appear in your trade list and be included in summaries.

Saved trades can be edited later if corrections are needed.
