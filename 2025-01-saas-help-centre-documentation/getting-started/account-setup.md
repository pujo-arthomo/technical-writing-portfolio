# Account Setup Guide

This guide explains how to set up your account and prepare it for first use.

## Step 1: Create Your Account
Create an account using your email address and a secure password.

After registration, verify your email to activate your account.

## Step 2: Complete Basic Profile Settings
Fill in your preferred timezone and trading session preferences.

These settings ensure that trade timestamps and summaries are displayed correctly.

## Step 3: Confirm Your Dashboard Access
Once setup is complete, you will be taken to your main dashboard.

If the dashboard does not load, refresh the page or sign out and sign back in.
