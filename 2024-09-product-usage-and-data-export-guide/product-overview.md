# Product Overview

## What This Product Does

This product helps users collect, manage, and review structured data generated through routine interactions.

Rather than acting as a complex analytics platform, it focuses on:
- consistent data collection
- predictable workflows
- easy access to stored information

The product is designed to support everyday operational needs rather than advanced data analysis.

---

## Core Capabilities

At a high level, the product allows users to:
- submit structured information through predefined inputs
- store records in a centralized system
- export collected data for review or reporting

Each capability is intentionally simple to reduce setup and learning time.

---

## What This Product Does Not Do

To set clear expectations, it is important to note that the product:
- does not perform advanced analytics
- does not automatically interpret or validate business logic
- does not replace dedicated reporting tools

These boundaries help users decide when the product fits their needs and when additional tools may be required.
