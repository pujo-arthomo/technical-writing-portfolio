# Common Use Cases

This section describes common scenarios where users apply the product in their daily work.

The examples focus on realistic usage rather than ideal or edge-case behavior.

---

## Reviewing Routine Submissions

Teams often use the product to review routine submissions collected over time.

In this scenario:
- data is submitted consistently
- reviewers focus on completeness and clarity
- exported data supports manual review

The product reduces the need for ad-hoc data gathering.

---

## Preparing Data for Reporting

Users may export data periodically to:
- create internal reports
- perform manual checks
- share summaries with other teams

The exported files are structured to be readable and compatible with common tools such as spreadsheets or simple scripts.

---

## Supporting Cross-Team Collaboration

Because the data format is predictable, teams can share exported files without extensive explanation.

This makes the product useful in environments where multiple roles interact with the same information.
